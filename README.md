## localization identifier

### Nintendo (Japan)

- ja: Japan - Japanese

### Nintendo of Europe

- de: Germany - German
- en: UK/AU - English
- es-es: Spain - Spanish
- fr-fr: France - French
- it: Italia - Italian

### Nintendo of America

- en: US - English
- es-419: Latin America/Caribbean - Spanish
- fr-ca: Canada - French
